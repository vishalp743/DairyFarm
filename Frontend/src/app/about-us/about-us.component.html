About Sandeep Dairy Farm & timeless Legacy.

Established by Dilip Chorghe in Mulshi, Sandeep Dairy Farm blends tradition with innovation. Beginning with Mahadu Chorghe's single cow, the legacy expanded to 70 cows under Diwanji's vision.

Today, emphasizing hygiene and ethics, they offer 20 milk and 30 sweet products with major outlets in Pirangut and Kothrud. Committed to excellence, they intertwine ancestral values with modernity.
